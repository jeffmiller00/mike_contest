Rules.

Each week of the NFL playoffs you choose either the side against the spread or the over/under for every game.

You get to "boost" one of those games per week to count double.

If multiple entries are submitted, I will use the last submission.

Round 1 each game is 1 point, Round 2 each game is 2 points, Round 3 each game is 3 points toward for the cumulative score.

Entry is $10/wk (no Super Bowl) so $30 total paid up front. Each week 25% is paid out to whomever has the most wins and the final 25% to the overall winner.
